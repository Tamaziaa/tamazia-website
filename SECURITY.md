# Security policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Email **security@tamazia.co.uk** (alias forwards to founder@). Encrypt with the PGP key at `/.well-known/security.txt`.

We respond within 24 hours and aim to publish a coordinated disclosure within 30 days. Acknowledgments at [/security-acknowledgments/](https://tamazia.co.uk/security-acknowledgments/).

## Scope

In scope:
- tamazia.co.uk and tamazia.in (web)
- Cloudflare Pages Functions at tamazia.co.uk/api/*
- Email infrastructure (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)

Out of scope:
- Third-party services (Cal.com, Resend, Cloudflare, GitHub) → report to those vendors directly
- Social engineering of Tamazia staff
- Physical attacks
- Denial-of-service via volume

## Bug bounty

Tamazia does not currently operate a paid bug bounty programme. We acknowledge contributions on the Acknowledgments page.
