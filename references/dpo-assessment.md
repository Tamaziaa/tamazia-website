# DPO Assessment · 2026-05-05

## Article 37 UK GDPR analysis

A Data Protection Officer is mandatory only if any of the following apply to Tamazia:

(a) Tamazia is a public authority or body — NO
(b) Tamazia's core activities consist of processing operations which require regular and systematic monitoring of data subjects on a large scale — NO. Tamazia's core activities are advisory delivery to a small client roster (less than 50 active clients per year) plus modest inbound contact form traffic.
(c) Tamazia's core activities consist of processing on a large scale of special-category data or data relating to criminal convictions — NO. Tamazia processes business contact information only.

Conclusion: A statutory DPO is not required. A nominated point of contact for data subject requests is documented in the privacy notice as founder@tamazia.co.uk.
