# Article 14 Notice Template · 2026-05-05

When Tamazia obtains personal data from a source other than the data subject (e.g. enrichment from Apollo, Hunter, Common Room, ZoomInfo, scraped public sources), UK GDPR Article 14 requires us to notify the data subject within one month, or at the latest at the time of the first communication.

## Template (paste into the first email Tamazia sends)

Subject: We have your contact details · how and why · Tamazia

Hello [first name],

I am writing because Tamazia obtained your business contact details from a publicly available source or a verified data provider, and Article 14 of the UK GDPR requires me to tell you so within one month of that retrieval.

What we hold: your business email address, your name, your role, and the company you currently work at.

Where we obtained it: [Apollo / Hunter / Common Room / public LinkedIn profile / company website].

Why we are using it: to evaluate whether Tamazia's lawyer-led SEO and compliance content services would be useful to you. Lawful basis: Article 6(1)(f) Legitimate Interest.

How long we keep it: until you ask us to delete it, or until 24 months after the last meaningful interaction, whichever is earlier.

Your rights: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and lodging a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office.

To exercise any of these rights, reply to this email or write to founder@tamazia.co.uk.

To stop receiving any further outreach from Tamazia, reply STOP.

Best regards,
[Sender name]
[Sender title]
Tamazia

## When to use

Every cold outreach email from a Tamazia persona that uses third-party-sourced data must carry this notice in the first contact. After the first contact, ongoing communications are governed by the recipient's response.

## Cal.com booking flow

When you book a strategy call via /book/, the booking calendar is provided by Cal.com Inc. (US, DPF-certified). Cal.com receives your name, email, optional notes, and chosen time-slot. Cal.com is a sub-processor under our DPA and is bound to UK GDPR Standard Contractual Clauses for the international transfer. The booking record is forwarded to Tamazia's Cloudflare Workers KV store via signed webhook (HMAC-SHA256). Retention: 2 years from booking date. You may request deletion at any time via founder@tamazia.co.uk.
