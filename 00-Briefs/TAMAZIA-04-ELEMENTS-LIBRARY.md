# TAMAZIA · 100 UNIQUE ELEMENTS CATALOGUE
**Curated from ~180 brainstormed ideas across 10 categories**
**Each element has: name, description, placement on site, and why it survives the "would a magic-circle Managing Partner take this seriously" test**

All elements obey the brand system (Playfair + Inter + Freight Big Pro; ivory/obsidian/oxblood/gold/pearl; GSAP motion; container queries; clamp fluid typography).

---

## CATEGORY 01 · TYPOGRAPHY AS INSTRUMENT (10 elements)

**1.1 Kinetic H1 three-line stagger.** H1 assembles line by line on load at 180ms stagger, Playfair variable weight animating 400→700. Hero only.

**1.2 Hanging punctuation.** Pull quotes and section intros have their opening quotes hang into the margin. True typographer's detail. Pull quotes across hero, Why Us, Case Studies, Pricing.

**1.3 Drop cap in Playfair.** Founder bios and FAQ answers open with oversized drop cap. Founder section, FAQ section.

**1.4 Tabular lining numerals for statistics.** Every statistic on the site uses Playfair tabular lining so numerals align vertically. Why Us stats, Pricing prices, Report engine metrics, Case Studies.

**1.5 Roman numerals as section markers.** Sections labelled "I. II. III. IV." in Playfair italic, gold. Used on Case Studies, Sectors cards, Pricing tiers, FAQ categories.

**1.6 Eyebrow caps 0.15em tracking.** Every section sub-heading uses Inter 11px uppercase with 0.15em letter-spacing for editorial gravitas. All sections.

**1.7 Gold corner brackets on pull quotes.** 2px gold hairline brackets frame the top-left and bottom-right corner of each pull quote, 32px legs. Hero quote, Sectors closing quote, Case Studies closing quote, FAQ editorial breaks.

**1.8 Freight Big Pro oversized numerals.** Oversized single stats (96-220px) in Freight Big Pro. Why Us, Case Studies, Pricing, Report engine.

**1.9 Variable weight hover on CTAs.** CTA text inside Playfair goes from weight 400 to 600 on hover over 200ms. Signals precision, not enthusiasm.

**1.10 Dateline strip above header.** Top thin strip with "LONDON · DUBAI · NEW YORK · PARIS · MEMBER, CHARTERED INSTITUTE OF ARBITRATORS" in Inter caps 11px. Masthead feel.
