# Tamazia Brand Voice

## Five absolute red lines (machine enforced)

1. **No Indian jurisdiction.** Never name Indian regulator, law, court, or example. Tamazia does not serve the Indian market.
2. **"200+" everywhere.** Marquee labels read "200+ regulatory frameworks reviewed per campaign". Never "200" bare. Never "45". Never any other count.
3. **"Aman Pareek" always capitalised.** Capital A, capital P. In code, comments, alt text, anywhere it ships.
4. **No em dashes used as pauses.** Period or ` · ` interpunct only. Hard rule. Enforced by patch-dist.js verification check 6 at deploy time.
5. **No consumer-SaaS playbooks.** No "Get started free", no virality framing, no growth-hack copy. Reference: Patek Philippe / Hermès institutional editorial.
