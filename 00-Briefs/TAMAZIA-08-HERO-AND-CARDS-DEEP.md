# TAMAZIA · HERO GLOBE + 6-CARD 3D ADDENDUM

---

## UPFRONT CHALLENGE

Using 30-40 third-party logos on the site is a retail pattern that will actively damage Tamazia's peer positioning with the top-100 clientele. Regulator crests and peer firm logos are trademarked and mostly unusable without licence. The luxury equivalent is **typographic reference**. Every element in this brief uses typography, not logo plates, for law/regulator/peer references. Logos are reserved for items Tamazia legitimately has rights to display.

## LAYER 1 · CLIENT PERSPECTIVE DEEP-DIVE

For each archetype: what they fear, who they compare themselves to, what credibility markers they respect.

### Archetype 1 · Managing Partner, Magic Circle / US Elite Law Firm
Age 48-62. Reads FT, Chambers Review. Decisions measured in professional indemnity exposure and Law Society standing.

- **Fears:** SRA investigation, Bar Association advertising review, professional negligence claim, reputational hit in Chambers Review.
- **Compares self against:** Clifford Chance, Kirkland, Latham, Slaughter and May, Freshfields.
- **Respects:** Chambers Global / UK / HNW rankings, Legal 500 bands, Queen's Counsel titles, King's College / Oxford / Cambridge / Harvard / Yale credentials.
- **Signal they respond to:** "LLM, International Business Law, King's College London." Beats "growth hacker" by order of magnitude.

### Archetype 2 · Group CMO, Luxury Hotel Brand
Age 42-55. Global hospitality experience. Decisions measured in direct-booking revenue and Forbes Travel Guide ratings.

- **Fears:** OTA commission erosion (Booking.com, Expedia), Tripadvisor crisis, losing Forbes 5-star review.
- **Compares against:** Aman Resorts, Mandarin Oriental, Four Seasons, Rosewood, Belmond, Cheval Blanc.
- **Respects:** Forbes Travel Guide 5-Star, Michelin Keys, AA Rosettes (UK), World's 50 Best Hotels, Condé Nast Johansens.
- **Signal they respond to:** case study showing OTA-to-direct shift with verified numbers.

### Archetype 3 · General Counsel / VP Communications, Listed / Pre-IPO Company
Age 45-58. Deep SEC / FCA / DFSA exposure. Decisions measured in disclosure risk.

- **Fears:** SEC Reg FD violation, FCA financial promotion breach, shareholder class action, SEC inquiry, insider trading appearance.
- **Compares against:** other listed-company IR and communications operations.
- **Respects:** SEC-listed attorney credentials, securities law bar passage, CG Oncology-type verified case studies.
- **Signal they respond to:** "We handled NYSE IPO digital coverage with SEC disclosure applied to every word. Shares nearly doubled. Zero compliance incidents."

### Archetype 4 · CEO / Medical Director, Private Clinic or Healthcare Group
Age 40-55. Medical + commercial background. Decisions measured in patient volume and CQC inspection outcome.

- **Fears:** CQC inspection downgrade, GMC complaint, MHRA advertising breach, Google E-E-A-T rank loss, HIPAA audit (US).
- **Compares against:** Cleveland Clinic, Mayo, HCA Healthcare, BUPA Cromwell, The London Clinic.

- **Signal they respond to:** "Google scrutinises medical content harder than any other sector. E-E-A-T + HIPAA + MHRA enforced as a unit."

### Archetype 5 · MD / Developer, Luxury Real Estate Portfolio
Age 45-65. Transaction values in 7-9 figures. Decisions measured in prime buyer reach and RERA compliance (UAE).

- **Fears:** RERA Trakheesi permit breach, FCA financial promotion violation on investment-grade property, losing HNW buyer cycle to competitor.

### Archetype 6 · Principal / CEO, Private Bank or Family Office
Age 45-60. Deep regulatory experience. Decisions measured in assets under management and FCA COBS 4 cleanliness.

### Archetype 7 · Partner, Institutional Investment Bank or Big Four Legal

## LAYER 3 · HERO VISUAL ARCHITECTURE · 10 CONCEPTS BRAINSTORMED

Concept brief: the hero needs one cohesive composition that embodies "international regulatory + SEO + four continents" in a single glance. The globe idea the user described is the anchor. The regulatory constellation + verified clients + watermark all fold in.

### Concept 1 · The Globe with Orbital Constellation (RECOMMENDED)

Centred on obsidian: slowly rotating gold wireframe globe (not solid — continents as gold lines only, oceans transparent). 360° rotation over 90 seconds. Inside/around the globe:

- **Four city anchors lit in gold**: London, Dubai, New York, Paris. Small gold pin dots with `LDN` / `DXB` / `NYC` / `PAR` callouts in Inter 11px caps that appear on hover.
- **Three verified-client markers** as slightly larger gold pins: Mumbai (Kamat), Irvine CA (CG Oncology), Dubai (Meraas). On hover, a small editorial caption slides in: `KAMAT HOTELS · NSE-LISTED HOSPITALITY · 2023-`.
- **Regulatory constellation orbiting the globe**: 12 regulation callouts (SRA, BAR, DIFC, GDPR, FCA, MHRA, HIPAA, EU AI ACT, SEC, DFSA, RERA, ASA) as Inter 11px caps gold text drifting slowly in an elliptical orbit. On hover any item: small editorial tooltip gives one-line definition.
- **Below the globe (integrated into composition)**: Tamazia Scales of Law and Search sculpture small, as a grounding anchor, made of polished gold + pearl. Connects the globe to the firm's practice.
- **Watermark layer at back**: Tamazia horse monogram at 3% opacity, diagonal diamond lattice 120px intervals.
- **Credential strip overlaid near bottom**: `KING'S COLLEGE LONDON · CHARTERED INSTITUTE OF ARBITRATORS · AMERICAN BAR ASSOCIATION · GOOGLE PARTNER · META BUSINESS PARTNER` Inter 11px caps 0.15em tracking gold.
- **H1 positioned left** (as per Build Bible): "Outrank competitors. / Master regulators. / One agency." Globe composition occupies the right 40%.

### Concept 2 · Scales on a Globe Pedestal

Suspended gold objects — scale, gavel, stethoscope, keystone, bell, compass — slowly rotating on invisible threads. Central: Tamazia horse. Complex but beautiful. Risk: cluttered.

### Concept 7 · Stack of Law Books Rotating
Gold-spined antique law books labelled with regulations (SRA, FCA, MHRA, HIPAA, GDPR), slowly rotating like a carousel. Library-as-authority signal. Risk: reads too generic "lawyer stock imagery."

### Concept 8 · Sunrise Horizon
Gold horizon line over obsidian with rising sun or moon. Minimalist, institutional (Berkshire Hathaway annual report vibe). Elegant but too passive for a hero asset.

### Concept 9 · Split Diptych
Left half: scales + law book. Right half: magnifying glass + search cursor. Between: gold hairline divider. Two-panel composition for "legal + SEO." Clean but less unified.

### Concept 10 · Wireframe Arches (Architecture)
Three gold arches forming a suspended structure, each arch labelled with a client jurisdiction. Minimalist architectural form, no figurative object. Bold but risks abstraction.

### Curated selection

**Concept 1 is the winner.** It answers all five of Aman's asks in one composition:
1. ✅ Globe present and central.
2. ✅ Regulatory constellation (web of laws) as orbital elements.
3. ✅ Verified client markers + credential strip (web of peer affiliations).
4. ✅ Small headlines on hover reveal each.
5. ✅ Watermark at back.

Supporting runner-up: **Concept 2** (Scales on Globe Pedestal) as a simpler fallback if Claude Design can't generate a clean wireframe globe with rotating orbits in first pass.

## LAYER 4 · EXECUTION ROADMAP + FREE TOOL OPTIONS

### Step 1 · Asset production decision

Three free routes for the 7 × 3D illustrations (1 hero globe + 6 sector cards):

**Route A · Claude Design (default, no new tooling).**
Generate all 7 in a single Claude Design batch session using the specs above. Claude Design outputs transparent-background PNG + CSS-driven animation. Zero external cost. Aesthetic ceiling: good. Cohesion risk: medium (mitigated by batching).

**Route B · Spline.design (free tier).**
Visual 3D builder, drag-drop + prompt-based. Exports interactive 3D scenes embeddable via iframe. Zero cost at free tier (up to 2 GB). Aesthetic ceiling: very good with practice. Learning curve: 2-4 hours. Aman-friendly (no code).
- URL: https://spline.design
- Alternative: Vectary (similar, also free tier).

**Route C · AI 3D generators (free trials).**
Text-to-3D tools that produce exportable GLB/GLTF:
- **Meshy AI** (https://meshy.ai): ~200 free credits at signup, text + image to 3D.
- **Luma AI Genie** (https://lumalabs.ai): free tier with monthly credits, text to 3D.
- **Rodin / Hyperhuman** (https://hyperhuman.deemos.com): text to 3D, free credits.
- **Trellis by Microsoft** (https://github.com/Microsoft/TRELLIS): open-source, runs locally on GPU.

Aesthetic ceiling: inconsistent. Quality varies massively by object type. Luxury sculptural objects (gold on obsidian) are harder than cartoonish ones. Use only as a last resort if Routes A and B fail.

**Recommendation:** Route A first (Claude Design). If three iterations do not produce cohesive luxury quality, fall back to Route B (Spline). Route C only if both stall.

## APPENDIX · HONEST RISKS

1. **Claude Design may not produce a clean wireframe globe with rotating orbital text on first attempt.** Wireframe 3D with animated overlay text is demanding. Fallback: build the globe in Spline (15-min template available), embed via iframe, layer orbital text as absolutely-positioned HTML elements above the iframe. This hybrid works reliably.

2. **Regulatory tooltip text library requires maintenance.** Regulations update. Build the text library as a JSON file in the Astro project so updates are one-file edits.

3. **Verified client permissions may take weeks.** Launch with typographic references first ("NSE-LISTED HOSPITALITY GROUP · NYSE-LISTED BIOTECH · DIFC REAL ESTATE PORTFOLIO") and swap in logos as permissions arrive. Do not delay launch for permissions.

4. **Globe at mobile width will need to shrink or reconfigure.** Spec a mobile alternative: globe scales to 280px diameter, orbital text collapses into a static ring below globe, hover tooltips become tap-to-reveal.

5. **Screen readers and the globe.** Fully accessible via `aria-label` on the globe container and each orbital label rendered as semantic text with `aria-hidden` on the purely decorative SVG lines.
