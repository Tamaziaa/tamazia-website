## BD-1 · Authority tier upsell — 20% discount with "Preferred Partner Rate" framing

**Decision:** 20% (per Aman's explicit directive) BUT wrapped in premium "Preferred Partner Rate for 6-month strategic engagement" language, NEVER displayed as "20% off."

**Research base (11 sources):**
1. [ConsultingSuccess — Consulting Rates 2025](https://www.consultingsuccess.com/consulting-rates) — 10-15% typical retainer discount in premium consulting
2. [Double Your Freelancing — Retainer Agreements](https://doubleyourfreelancing.com/freelancers-guide-client-retainer-agreements/) — 10-20% range standard; 85-90% of project rate
3. [Phoenix Strategy Group — Upselling Tactics](https://www.phoenixstrategy.group/blog/7-upselling-tactics-for-saas-revenue-growth) — priority-access framing > discount framing
4. [Wayfront — Agency Retainer Pricing](https://wayfront.com/blog/retainer-pricing) — explicit warning: "Positioning retainers as discounted rates can position your retainer as a commodity rather than a premium service"
5. [ConsultFees — Consulting Retainers 2026](https://consultfees.com/blog/consulting-retainers) — 3/6/12-month commitment tiers standard
6. [Invoice Bloom — Consultant Pricing Models](https://invoicebloom.io/blog/freelance-consultant-pricing-models) — retainer math (rate × hours × 0.85-0.90)
7. [Seven Figure Agency — Digital Agency Retainers](https://sevenfigureagency.com/reliable-insights-into-digital-agency-retainer-pricing-models/) — agency-specific retainer structures
8. [Focus Digital — Upsell Conversion 2025](https://focus-digital.co/average-upsell-conversion-rate-2025-report/) — 60-70% success upselling existing vs 5-20% new customer
9. [Dan Siepen — SaaS Upsell Strategies](https://www.dansiepen.io/growth-checklists/saas-upsell-product-upgrade-email-strategies) — segmented campaigns drive 760% more revenue
10. [Solo Ledger — Freelance Retainer Agreements](https://www.thesololedger.com/freelance-retainer-agreements/) — trigger-based framing
11. [School of Anti-Workaholics — Pricing Formula](https://schoolofantiworkaholics.substack.com/p/the-only-pricing-formula-you-need) — premium pricing psychology

**Reasoning:** Research is unanimous on one point — displaying retainer pricing as a discount destroys premium positioning. Aman specified 20%. The resolution: keep the 20% value but wrap it entirely in premium language. Client never sees "20% OFF." They see "Authority tier at £3,600/month for a 6-month strategic engagement" with the standard £4,500 anchored next to it. The savings are visible but positioned as structural (continuity + founder time commitment), not promotional. This preserves both Aman's commercial directive AND the research-validated premium register.

**Copy specification for the upsell card:**

```
PREFERRED PARTNER RATE · 6-MONTH STRATEGIC ENGAGEMENT

Authority tier, £3,600/month for six months.
Standard rate £4,500/month.

The reduced rate is structural. Six months of strategic continuity 
means your Regulatory Analyst, AI Search Engineer, and Legal 
Content Strategist are not rebuilding context each month. The 
founder reviews every deliverable. Scope locked after your audit 
review; no surprises after month one.
```
