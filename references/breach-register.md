# Personal data breach register

This file records personal data breaches as defined in UK GDPR Article 4(12). Maintained by the controller, Tamazia Pvt Ltd.

A "personal data breach" means a breach of security leading to the accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, unauthorised disclosure of, or access to, personal data transmitted, stored or otherwise processed.

## Notification protocol

1. **Detection** · Anyone who suspects a breach emails dpo@tamazia.co.uk with subject line beginning "[BREACH]".
2. **Assessment within 4 hours** · Controller (or DPO designate) classifies severity and likely risk to data subjects.
3. **ICO notification within 72 hours** if the breach is likely to result in a risk to the rights and freedoms of natural persons (UK GDPR Art 33). Use the ICO online breach reporting form at https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/report-a-breach/personal-data-breach/.
4. **Subject notification without undue delay** if the breach is likely to result in a high risk to rights and freedoms (UK GDPR Art 34). Use email if address is on file; postal letter if not.
5. **Register entry** · Controller adds a row to the table above with all 8 columns within 7 days of closure.

## Internal breach categories (severity)

- **C1 critical** · Unauthorised disclosure of identifiable personal data of >100 data subjects, or any disclosure of special category data (Article 9), or financial data, or data of children. Mandatory ICO notification + subject notification.
- **C2 significant** · Unauthorised disclosure or loss of personal data of 1-100 subjects, with risk of harm. ICO notification likely.
- **C3 minor** · Internal-only exposure with quick containment, no third-party disclosure, no risk of harm. Recorded but not externally reported.
- **C4 averted** · Suspected breach investigated and confirmed not a breach. Recorded for audit.

## Audit trail

This register is reviewed at the start of each calendar quarter by the controller. Any open entries are escalated; closed entries archived to references/breach-register-archive-YYYY-Qn.md.
