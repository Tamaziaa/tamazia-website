How do boutique law firms build niche entity authority for AI search citation in 2026?

Niche entity authority for a boutique law firm is built on three foundations. First, deep FAQPage schema: 12 to 20 Q&A pairs covering the exact buyer queries for the boutique's specific practice area, structured to match how buyers phrase questions to ChatGPT and Perplexity rather than to Google.
